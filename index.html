Working together with the Community!

We are an experienced team of Developers & Designers actively developing the Buzzcoin Project through community engagement.

Buzzcoin is being actively developed as a Proof of Stake (PoS) coin, with a current interest rate of 100% per month. This makes Buzzcoin one of the most profitable PoS coins available. The total coin supply is 2,000,000,000 BUZZ. We are actively working against Buzzcoin becoming a pump and dump coin by using 25% TXFees capped at 50,000 BUZZ.
